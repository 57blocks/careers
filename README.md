# 57Blocks Careers

This repository serves as the official job board for [57Blocks](https://57blocks.com). All open positions are published and managed through [GitHub Issues](../../issues).

## About 57Blocks

57Blocks is a global engineering services firm, spun out of Adobe and operating since 2018. We help visionary founders and forward-thinking enterprises accelerate and scale their Product Development Life Cycle (PDLC).

**What we do:**

- **AI Engineering** — Applied AI systems, model design & deployment, AI Copilot Suite, vertical AI applications (Legal, Finance, Robotics, Healthcare, Agriculture), and proprietary DataOps tooling.
- **Web3 / Blockchain Engineering** — Layer 1/L2 systems, smart contracts (EVM, Solana, Stellar, Sui), DApp development, and cross-chain infrastructure.

**By the numbers:**

- 100+ products launched
- 200+ team members
- 30+ blockchain chains supported with 99.9% uptime
- Trusted by Google, Apple, Fanatics, Autodesk, Story Protocol, Stellar, Paxos, and Y Combinator / a16z-backed startups
